The construction of the design of this website started with a research in the a The WWW Awards website, and there was two thigs that called my attention on the Grette Website (one of the winners of the WWW award), it’s simplicity on design and the “hamburger” menu where things that I loved. This was the first step for me to start my sketches (these where the first bash of sketches, which I unfortunately lost, and won’t be able to show you) for the design and then start coding my website.

The Website That Inspired Me

After the first try of my website, I asked my friend, Henrique Mendes, for his opinion because I know he would tell me the truth and wouldn’t say stuff just to make me happy. After hearing his opinion, I realized that the way that I’ve done my website was one that I enjoyed myself, so, like a computer, I restarted everything from scratch, I redid the sketches.

When I finished the new bash of sketches, I started coding again, and after I asked for my friend’s opinion again, I has getting somewhere, because finally I has doing something with the design that I like. But this time I draw on the sketches the “hamburger” menu, this was something that I didn’t knew how to do without Java, because one of my objectives for this website was to try not to use Java, so I wwnt to CodePen to try and see if someone (that someone called Grant Vinson) was able to the menu that I wanted without using Java, and after a while I could see that someone did, so I decided to use their code on my website.

To conclude, after finishing my website, I was proud of it, I did have to use someone else’s code, but in the end, I credited when credit was due. Still, I felt that I did a good job with my website, and I hope that whoever is reading this thinks the same.
